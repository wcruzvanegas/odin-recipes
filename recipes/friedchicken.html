Ingredients

  • 4 lb. chicken, cut up
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 quarts veg oil

Steps

  1. Cut chicken. Put flour in bag with seasoning, paprika, salt/pepper.
  2. Dip chicken in buttermilk, then in bag w/ flower. Put on cookie tray, cover with wax paper. Let flower get to paste consistency.
  3. Fill skillet. Half oil half water. Make boil. Brown chicken in hot oil. Reduce heat and cover when brown. Let cook for 30 min. Repeat until crispy.
  4. Drain onto paper towels. Repeat steps if need be.
